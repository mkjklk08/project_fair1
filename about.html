Jambyl Innovation High College (JIHC) is a college in the Jambyl region of Kazakhstan. It was created to give students good education and skills for jobs in technology, accounting .

JIHC works with local businesses so students can learn skills that are needed in real jobs. These partnerships help students get practical experience and be ready for work after they finish high college.

Today, JIHC is known for its modern programs and strong focus on preparing students for their careers. The college has up-to-date equipment and programs that match the latest job needs in technology and other fields.
